Our History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiast John Doe, Whitewater Rafting Adventures began as a small operation with just two rafts. Over the years, we've grown to become one of the most respected rafting companies in the area, with a fleet of 15 rafts and a team of 25 certified guides. We've safely taken over 50,000 adventurers down the river while maintaining our commitment to environmental conservation and safety.
